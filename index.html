About Flanders Make

Flanders Make is the research centre for industrial innovation. We help companies of all sizes introduce cutting-edge technology solutions in their manufacturing process, at scale. We do it by matching the businesses with research teams who will develop a science-based, objective answer to your question. With over a decade worth of data generated through thousands of projects and an active exploration of the newest technologies, we offer you access to a profound knowledge base and guide you through every step of the way. We research, we analyse, we prototype, and we test to deliver a verified solution. At the end of the process, you will receive comprehensive guidelines that will enable you to move forward with implementation. One problem. One thousand brains to solve it, narrowed down to one team, dedicated to your challenge. Let’s make it possible.
